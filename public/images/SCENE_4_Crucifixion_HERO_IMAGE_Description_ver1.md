## Description

Three men hang crucified on Golgotha as Roman soldiers execute their orders with mechanical precision, but the centurion Longinus discovers that systematic cruelty cannot touch the peace radiating from the center cross—a moment when he begins to perceive that the apparatus of state violence meets something incomprehensibly larger than itself. His vision, which has been failing, suddenly becomes secondary to a deeper seeing that will shatter everything he understands about his own complicity.
